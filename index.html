Define Your Product

Select the category that best represents your product or service. This helps our algorithm calculate more accurate estimates based on your industry's typical behavior.

2

Interpreting Results

Key Metrics

  • Estimated Reach: Number of unique people who will see your ad.
  • Expected Clicks: Direct interactions with your ads.
  • Projected Conversions: Completed actions (purchases, registrations, etc.).
  • Estimated ROI: Projected return on investment.

Recommended Strategies

  • Budget Optimization: Based on simulation results, consider reallocating your budget toward platforms with better performance for your product type and audience.
  • Multi-platform Approach: Diversifying your strategy across multiple platforms typically yields better results than concentrating all resources on a single one.
  • Periodic Analysis: Run simulations regularly to adjust your strategy as market trends and consumer behavior change.
